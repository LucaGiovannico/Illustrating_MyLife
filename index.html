About me and my work

Hi, I'm Luca and I'm an illustrator. I love everything that has to do with technology, which is why one of my goals is to become a web developer. I have an instagram profile where I publish all my creations, come and see them.

Illustrating_mylife

Software

My works are created with Adobe illustrator so that the illustration is created in a vector manner and there are no problems when enlarging.

Colors

I use bright colors so that they immediately stand out and give a feeling of lightness and carefreeness to whoever is looking at it.

Inspiration

Having had a passion for anime, manga and cartoons since I was a child, I take inspiration above all from the latter.

Work on commission

I also work on commission. If you like my work, contact me privately on Instagram and we'll have a chat.
